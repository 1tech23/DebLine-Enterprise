Who We Are

DebLine Industries is known for it's creation of the phenominal data transfer system (DTS), known as HighLight. HighLight DTS 1 uses what is called a quantum supersystem to simultaneously send and recieve data to and from individual systems. It is a satellite, as such technology would overheat if it were centered on Earth, rather than in the cold vacuum of space. It's command station is a computer that utilizes a system called DebLine OS.

DebLine OS is a Linux-based computer system that utilizes both software and graphics. It is extremely fast, and it only requires the use of a few megabytes of data to run. It can also be installed on nearly any comuter, whether it's the old Apple II (if you know what that looked like), or a three-day old Windows laptop.
